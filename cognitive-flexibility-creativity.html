Study mode

Dual Pathway Model

Creativity can emerge through broad switching or deep sustained work. The strongest creators can regulate both.

Model

Flexibility Pathway

Main move
Shift across categories, associations, and frames.
Best use
Early ideation, brainstorming, reframing, and finding remote possibilities.
Risk
Too much switching can become shallow if no idea receives sustained attention.

Persistence Pathway

Main move
Explore one problem space with steady, systematic effort.
Best use
Refining ideas, testing constraints, and improving quality or usefulness.
Risk
Too much persistence can lock thinking into familiar categories.
